About our program

At After School Boca Raton, we are committed to creating a nurturing and engaging environment where children ages 5-12 can thrive. Founded by Julia and Niko Kovalevich—a world champion in Muay Thai—our program combines a dynamic mix of sports, arts, and chess, all designed to inspire and empower young minds.

Highlights of Our Program

  • Personalized Growth: Our activities are designed to nurture each child's unique talents and interests.
  • Expert Coaching: Led by experienced coaches who prioritize your child's success in a supportive, family-like atmosphere.

Our mission is to help your child excel not only academically and athletically but also to build confidence, discipline, and vital social skills. With activities that encourage creativity and teamwork, children will develop their interests while forging lasting friendships. We provide daily transportation from local elementary schools, ensuring safety and convenience for busy families.
