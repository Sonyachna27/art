Our services that we offer

FREE School Transportation

Ensure safe and convenient transport for your child with our free daily pick-up service from local elementary schools. We provide comfortable, fully insured vehicles, prioritizing your child's safety as they travel to Artletics for fun after-school activities.

Martial Arts Classes

Boost your child's confidence through martial arts training at Artletics. Our classes teach essential self-defense skills while promoting discipline and respect. Kids learn to stand up to bullying and can participate in competitions as they work towards earning their black belts.

Art and Craft Activities

Foster your child's creativity with engaging art and craft projects at Artletics. Children explore various mediums, allowing them to express themselves and develop their artistic skills. These activities enhance fine motor skills and boost confidence as kids showcase their work

Chess Classes

Enhance your child's strategic thinking through chess classes at Artletics. Our sessions cover fundamental tactics and strategies, helping kids improve focus and concentration. Participants enjoy friendly matches, promoting teamwork and critical thinking skills.

About our program

At After School Boca Raton, we are committed to creating a nurturing and engaging environment where children ages 5-12 can thrive. Founded by Julia and Niko Kovalevich—a world champion in Muay Thai—our program combines a dynamic mix of sports, arts, and chess, all designed to inspire and empower young minds.

Highlights of Our Program

  • Personalized Growth: Our activities are designed to nurture each child's unique talents and interests.
  • Expert Coaching: Led by experienced coaches who prioritize your child's success in a supportive, family-like atmosphere.

Our mission is to help your child excel not only academically and athletically but also to build confidence, discipline, and vital social skills. With activities that encourage creativity and teamwork, children will develop their interests while forging lasting friendships. We provide daily transportation from local elementary schools, ensuring safety and convenience for busy families.
